A Nature Of Art Bangu Yilbara Collection: English To Hindi Video Transcription And Translation

/ A Nature Of Art Bangu Yilbara Collection: English To Hindi Video Transcription And Translation
Tat Banerjee [Last Updated: January 08, 2021]
Table Of Contents
  • Steps
  • Conclusion

We got this video from Museum of Contemporary Art Australia’s Youtube page This video is about Bangu Yilbara collection at the MCA. The collection is focused on art produced by the Aboriginal and Torres Straits Islander cultures.

The exhibition focuses on the diversity of Aboriginal and Torres Straits Islander culture, primarily by looking to the artists to define their work, as opposed to labelling the work ‘Aboriginal’ and boxing it into any particular style.

Below, we have the final video, after transcription into English, and translation into Hindi.

Steps

  1. Please open your browser and go to videotranslator.ai, and then click on the Login button. On successfully log-In into the application, select myTemplate, and create a new item mca.

  2. Upload the video and adjust it’s display size you want to display.

  3. Once you are done. Trigger the transcribe action. Select the language preferred for the video. People in this video has Australian accent, so we select language as English (Australia).

  4. Once transcription is complete, we quickly check it with human effort. And move forward to other step to Trigger Translate action to translate captions from English to Hindi.

  5. Once the Translate action is performed and complete, a pop up box appear with file name you want for that Hindi translation file. Input file name and click on Submit button to save the Translation.

  6. On successful completion of translation process open translated file and human check the Translation captions, to fix the sentences. Once this is done, we move to final step ‘Auto-Overlay’ for the captions to Overlay on video.

  7. That’s all. Thank you for reading this process and check out the final Auto-Overlay video here.

Conclusion

In this post, we have Transcribe and Translated English video content for Hindi Caption of ‘Museum of Contemporary Art Australia’. As the video now has Hindi captions, This Hindi Captions video will help to promote Museum Contemporary Art (MCA) and let people know more about Bangu Yilbara works.
