How To Translate YouTube Videos And Dominate SEO

The basic steps to translate a YouTube video are:

  1. Download the video to your computer.
  2. Upload the video into an AI transcription service.
  3. Get the transcript in an .srt file.
  4. Translate the transcript using an AI translation service.
  5. Embed the translation into your video.

You should only translate videos that you expect a good Return On Investment from - so you have a good feeling this video will work in a different language.

First, and this goes without saying, please translate your own YouTube videos. Why? Because of copyright. If its not your property, please do not steal from other content creators. Fair use is of couse good, but this blog post is for content creators who are looking to translate their own videos in YouTube.

In this blog post we cover how to download a video from YouTube, then we add a transcript, and then we translate the video. This is done so you can try this at home yourself.
