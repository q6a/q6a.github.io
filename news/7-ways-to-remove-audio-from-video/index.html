A video is always better with sound, but sometimes the video you want to share has unwanted background noises or music.

So, how can you remove the unwanted audio?

In this article, we will tell you 7 different ways to remove audio from your video!

7 Ways To Remove Audio From Video

1- Remove Audio From Video Using Adobe Premiere Pro

There are 2 ways to remove audio from your video in Adobe Premiere Pro.

The first method is used when you want to remove audio individually.

For example, you have 5 different clips and you only want to remove the audio of your first clip.

Here’s how to do it:

  1. Create a project in Adobe Premiere Pro and load your clip/s to the timeline.

  2. Select your clip and right-click on it.

    Remove Audio From Video Using Adobe Premiere Pro

  3. Click unlink. Your video and audio clip will be separated.

  4. Select your audio clip, press right-click, and select clear. Alternatively, you can press delete on your keyboard.

    Remove Audio From Individual Video Using Adobe Premiere Pro

The second method is used when you want to remove all of your video audio.

Here’s how to do it:

  1. Create a project in Adobe Premiere Pro and load your clip/s to the timeline.

  2. Click File > Export > Media or press cmd + M. Under the output name, untick the ‘Export Audio’ box.

    Remove Audio From All Video Using Adobe Premiere Pro

  3. Click export. Your video will be exported without the audio.

2 - Remove Audio From Video Using iMovie

If you use Mac and do not have Adobe Premiere Pro, iMovie is a good alternative to edit your video.

Here’s how to remove audio from your video in iMovie:

  1. Open iMovie and load your clip/s.

    Remove Audio From Video Using iMovie

  2. Right-click on your selected video and press detach audio or option + cmd + B.

  3. Select your audio clip and press delete.

3 - Remove Audio From Video Using VLC

  1. Open your VLC media player.

  2. Go to File > Convert/Stream.

  3. Load your selected clip.

    Remove Audio From Video Using VLC

  4. On the Choose Profile Tab, click on customise.

  5. Go to Audio Codec, untick the audio box and press apply.

    Untick The Audio Box In VLC

  6. Click save as file and your video new video will not have audio in it.

4 - Remove Audio From Video Using QuickTime Player

  1. Open QuickTime Player and load your clip.

  2. Go to Edit > Remove Audio.

    Remove Audio From Video Using QuickTime Player

  3. To save the video, go to File > Save, then choose the title and destination for your new video.

    Save Your New Video That Does Not Have Audio In QuickTime Player

5 - Remove Audio From Video Using iPhone

Removing the audio from your video on iPhone is super easy!

  1. Go to your Photos Gallery and select the video clip.

    Remove Audio From Video Using iPhone

  2. Click edit and tap the yellow speaker icon in the upper-left corner of the screen.

  3. Click save.

6 - Remove Audio From Video Using Samsung

  1. Open your Gallery and select your video clip.

  2. Click on the pencil (edit) icon and select the music (audio) icon.

    Remove Audio From Video Using Samsung

  3. Slide the video volume all the way to the left and click save. You will now have a new video that does not have audio in it.

7 - Remove Audio From Video Using Google Photos

  1. Open Google Photos and select your video clip.

  2. Click on edit and tap the speaker icon to mute the audio.

    Remove Audio From Video Using Google Photos

  3. Click save copy. You will now have a new video that does not have audio in it.
