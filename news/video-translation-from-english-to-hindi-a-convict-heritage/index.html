Video Translation From English To Hindi: A Convict Heritage

/ Video Translation From English To Hindi: A Convict Heritage
Tat Banerjee [Last Updated: January 08, 2021]
Table Of Contents
  • Steps
  • Conclusion

Today’s video is from the Hyde Park Barracks Museum’s Youtube page, specifically this video. We will convert this video from English to Hindi.

Hyde Park Barracks Museum is undergoing a refurbishment. This is expected to be complete late 2019. A large amount of new research by curators and researchers based at Hyde Park Barracks is also expected to be shown once the museum is reopened.

This new exhibit is about Australia’s convict heritage. The impact of convicts in early colonial Australia is documented, seen through the eyes of both the local aboriginal people, and the settlers.

Steps

  1. Please direct your browser to videotranslator.ai, and then click on the Login button. On successfully logging into the application, select myTemplate and create a new item called barracks1.

  2. Upload the video, and trigger the transcription action. Most of the speakers have generic Australian accents. So we select language need to transcribe as English (Australia).

  3. Once transcription is complete, we quickly check the transcription. This is to add capitalisation and grammar.

  4. We now click the + button to add the captions into the asset. The result is shown below.

  5. Now we want to translate to Hindi. As we have the captions, we can trigger a translation action to Hindi. Once translated, we check the Hindi translation and fixed up any errors.

  6. Now, we Add Caption to add the Hindi captions to our video. This is the resulting output.

  7. All Done! Thank you for taking the time to read this post.

Conclusion

In this post, we converted a video from Hyde Park Barracks from English to Hindi.

The value of the captions is not simply as an assist for someone who speaks Hindi. It is also for SEO. You would also translate the title, description and other metadata. Without this, even though the video now has Hindi captions, no one would be able to find it without proper Hindi SEO.
