Did you know that our AI can speak in over 180 languages and more than 120 differing dialects and different genders? It’s true! That’s one smart robot ;)

In this article we’re focusing on English and the four different dialects that you can choose from when using our app.

For those that have you used the app before, you will notice that we have renamed the “voices”. Previously we had them named as EN-IN-P-M1, or UK-S4-M which didn’t make a lot of sense for those who were unfamiliar with the technology.

Now, as you’ll see below the names are much more user friendly; we start with the country of origin, followed by the “age” of the voice, the gender and whether the AI’s voice is standard (S) or professional (P). Please note that standard (S) is an older version of the technology and so may sound a little more robotic than the professional (P) voices.

What Dialects are available in English?

We are constantly working on updating and adding new dialects to our app, at present we have four English dialects; Australian, Indian, British and American.

We’ve had each voice say the phrase “Life is what happens when you’re busy making other plans”, so you can hear the difference between each of the voices. Fun fact! Even though John Lennon is credited for the popular quote, it was actually writer and cartoonist Allen Saunders who published it in Reader’s Digest in January 1957.
