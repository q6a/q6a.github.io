We are super excited to share this news with you! Team VideoTranslator is growing. Welcome to Tessa, James and Yuriy. We are very excited to have you come aboard.

New Team Members!

Over the course of the last few months, Team VideoTranslator has grown! Now we are a team of five working on your favourite video translation app!

Tessa

Originally from Indonesia, Tessa Joana joins us in a Junior Digital Marketing role.

She blew away the competition with her video making skills, and has been hard at work, primarily on the Dandelions account.

We are super excited to have her, given she is very very good with digital content creation, and speaks quite a few languages, making her a perfect fit for Team VideoTranslator.

James

Hailing from wine delivery platform Troly, James has come on to help flesh out our VideoTranslator app.

Picking up the role of Principal Software Engineer, James is going to be owning the retail aspect of Team VideoTranslator’s value proposition.

He is very excited about the challenges ahead, and we are very excited to have him on the team.

Yuriy

Based out of Eastern Europe, Yuriy is a talented programmer who is going to be working with James on our app.

We have been lucky enough to have Yuriy working with us for a while, and thought it was time to formalize the relationship.

Thank you for coming on board Yuriy, and we very much appreciate the work so far and look forward to all the good things to come!

Conclusion

We are super excited about Team VideoTranslator growing, and very happy to welcome Tessa, James and Yuriy to the team!

Lots more is scheduled in for this year, and in a few weeks we hope to start getting a road map for development out.

We are super stoked about what the remainder of 2021 will bring, and 2022 looks to be when so much of the work pending/done this year will really hit the market.

Thank you to Tessa, James and Yuriy again and welcome! We are happy and excited to have you!
