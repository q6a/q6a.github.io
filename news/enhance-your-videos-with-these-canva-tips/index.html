Hello, peeps!

Welcome back to another Video Translator article where we give you tips and tricks regarding the media and translation world.

This time, we are going to tell you some tips that can enhance your videos with the help of the popular online graphic design app… Canva!

Canva is an Australian graphic design platform, used by creators to make social media graphics, presentations, posters, documents, and other visual content.

So… how can Canva enhance your videos?

Well, first things first.

To use Canva more efficiently, you would need to know some shortcuts. So, what are the shortcut keys available on Canva?

Canva Shortcut Keys

Essential Shortcut Keys You Should Know

Essential Shortcut Keys You Should Know On Canva

  1. Copy: Cmd/Ctrl + C

  2. Paste: Cmd/Ctrl + V

  3. Add a new page: Cmd/Ctrl + Return key

    Text Shortcut Keys

    Text Shortcut Keys On Canva

  4. Bold text: Cmd/Ctrl + B

  5. Italicized text: Cmd/Ctrl + I

  6. Underline text: Cmd/Ctrl + U

  7. Align text to the left: Cmd/Ctrl + shift + L

  8. Align text to the right: Cmd/Ctrl + shift + R

  9. Align text in the center: Cmd/Ctrl + shift + C

  10. Convert text to uppercase: Cmd/Ctrl + Shift + K

  11. Increase font size: Cmd/Ctrl + Shift + >

  12. Decrease font size: Cmd/Ctrl + Shift + <

    Elements Shortcut Keys

    Elements Shortcut Keys On Canva

  13. Group elements: Cmd/Ctrl + G

  14. Ungroup elements: Cmd/Ctrl + Shift + G

  15. Duplicate elements: Cmd/Ctrl + D

  16. Select all elements: Cmd/Ctrl + A

  17. Deselect element: ESC key

  18. Delete element/text: Delete key

  19. Send elements forward: Cmd/Ctrl + ]

  20. Send elements to front: Cmd/Ctrl + Option/Alt + ]

  21. Send elements backward: Cmd/Ctrl + [

  22. Send elements to back: Cmd/Ctrl +Option/Alt + [

    Graphics Shortcut Keys

    Graphics Shortcut Keys On Canva

  23. Add a circle: C key

  24. Add a line: L key

  25. Add a rectangle: R key

These shortcut keys will speed up your design process!

Quickly Upload Image

To upload an image, you normally would have to go to uploads > select your image. But, did you know that there are easier ways?

To easily upload your image on Canva, you can :

  • simply drag your image to your Canva page, or
  • Copy and paste your image to your page.

Now, you want to add some stickers to your video. You want it in blue, but you are struggling to find one.

How can you do it easily?

The answer is by filtering your elements or photos by colour!

Filter Elements Or Photos By Colour

  1. Go to elements and type down the element that you want to add.

  2. Click on the settings button and pick the color of your liking.

    Pick The Color Of Your Elements On Canva

  3. Choose your colour-filtered element!

    Filter Elements Or Photos By Colour On Canva

You have found a really cool image that you want to add to your video. However, you do not like the background of the photo. You also do not know how to use Photoshop.

How can you remove the background…?

Remove A Background

Here’s what you have to do to remove the background of your image:

  1. Select your image.

  2. Go to effects > remove background.

    Remove A Background On Canva

  3. Adjust the image by restoring or erasing your background according to your liking.

Note: this feature is only available for PRO users.

This time, you want to create those stylish thumbnails with a white silhouette. How do you do that…

It’s easy!

Add White Silhouette To Your Image

  1. Select your image.

  2. Go to effects > remove background.

  3. Duplicate your image and enlarge one of the images.

  4. Go to effects > Duotone and click on settings.

    Add White Silhouette To Your Image On Canva

  5. Change the shadow and highlight colours to white.

    Adjust The Highlights and Shadows Colour To White To Add White Silhouette To Your Image On Canva

  6. Make sure that the white image is behind the original image.

Your white silhouette is done, but… why does your design look so messy? How can you align it properly?

It can be quite hard to perfectly align your design on Canva.

Yes, you can sometimes see the pink lines that tell you when a design is aligned. However, it is not quite accurate.

So, here are the tips to help you align your design better!

Perfectly Align Your Design

  1. Go to File on the menu bar and click show ruler.

    How To Perfectly Align Your Design On Canva

  2. If you want a horizontal line, drag your cursor from the right side of the ruler towards the center and position it to where you want it to be.

  3. For the vertical line, drag your cursor from the top side of the ruler towards the center and position it to where you want it to be.

    Drag your Ruler To Perfectly Align Your Design On Canva

  4. To remove the ruler, simply drag the line out of your design page.

It is… TITLE TIME!

You want to create your video’s opening title and want to add a gradient background. But… There is no gradient background that suits your colour palette.

Don’t worry! You can customize your gradient background on Canva!

Add A Gradient Background

  1. Go to elements and type down gradient.

  2. Select the gradient graphics.

    Add A Gradient Background On Canva

  3. Adjust the color of your gradient.

  4. You can change the gradient location by flipping the image vertically and horizontally or manually rotate the image.

    Adjust The Colour Of Your Gradient On Canva

Okay, the gradient background is solved.

We are now moving on to creating your Title.

How can you design a fancy title?

Fear not! Canva has made it so easy to adjust your texts!

Here’s how 👇👇👇

Create Curved Text

Here is how you can make curved trendy texts.

  1. Type your text.

  2. On your menu bar, click effects.

    Click Effects To Create Curved Text On Canva

  3. Here, you can choose text styling. There are so many different stylings that you can choose, such as shadow, lift, hollow, splice, echo, glitch, and even neon!

    Choose Your Text Style On Canva

  4. Scroll down to shape and select curve.

  5. Adjust the curve.

    Adjust Your Text Curve On Canva

Your design is ready, your title is created.

Wait… It is not the right size!

What should you do?!

Resize your image size!

But… isn’t it hard? My design will all be messed up.

Nope! Not on Canva…

Resize Your Image Size

We all know how annoying it can be when you are already working on a project and the image size does not match the requirements.

In Canva, there is a magic tool called resize where you can change the size of your design.

To do this, you have to:

  1. Click on the resize button on the menu bar.

    How To Automatically Resize Your Image Size On Canva

  2. Either type down the new design height and width or choose from Canva’s size template.

    Adjust Your New Image Size On Canva

  3. Choose whether you want to copy and resize your project with the new design size or if you just want to resize your current design.

  4. Voila! Your design has now been resized!

Here’s a bonus tip if you are also using Canva to create PDF files…

Bonus Tip: How To Add Hyperlink For Your PDF

Ever looked at a PDF and wondered how you are able to be redirected to a website when you click on a text? The answer is by adding a hyperlink.

To do this, you have to:

  1. Select your text.

  2. Click on the link icon on your menu bar.

    Add Hyperlink For Your PDF

  3. Add your link and click apply.

    Type Your Link To Add Hyperlink For Your PDF

Note: the hyperlink will only work if you save it as a PDF.
